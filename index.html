The Property

An Estate on a Quiet Court.

Over 6,200 sq ft of executive living in prestigious Woodbridge Highlands — a renovated kitchen, walk-out basement, and a 27kW backup generator that runs the entire home.

Step inside to a sunken grand foyer with soaring cathedral ceilings, marble floors, and a beautifully crafted oak staircase with intricate wrought-iron railing — an architectural statement from the moment you arrive. The renovated kitchen features built-in top-of-the-line Miele appliances, a massive island with cabinets on both sides, and a walk-out to the south-facing concrete terrace overlooking an expansive private backyard. Formal living and dining rooms designed for the moments that matter. A separate main-floor den/home office offers privacy and convenience. The primary suite is a true retreat — spacious, private, and thoughtfully designed, with a spa-inspired ensuite featuring heated floors, a frameless two-person shower, and a deep soaker tub. The fully finished, open-concept walk-out basement features a gas fireplace, a 3-piece bath, a gym area, and a functional laundry or future kitchen setup — ideal for extended family or multi-generational living. The 27kW Generac generator provides backup power for the entire home. Steps to all amenities and quick access to Hwy 427, 400, and 407. Short drive to Pearson Airport.
